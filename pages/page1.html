CHEMICAL REACTION OF DIAMOND

Yield strength

Diamond has compressive yield strength of 130-140 GPa.Somewhat related to hardness is another mechanical property toughness, which is a material's ability to resist breakage from forceful impact. The toughness of natural diamond has been measured as 7.5-10 MPa.
